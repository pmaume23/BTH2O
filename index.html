About B2H2OPE

Bridge to H2OPE (B2H2OPE) is a non-profit organization dedicated to combating the water crisis in developing countries. B2H2OPE works with other NGOs, private donors, student organizations in the universities/colleges to fund and support water well projects throughout villages in Africa. Our intentions are to make caring for our underprivileged rural communities by increasing clean water accessibility, which saves lives and creates more opportunities.

"As a teacher, it was always dissapointing for me to see female students not attending school as their male peers. Lack of water in this area has significantly affected the educational perfomance of many girls"

----Gebe Medhin, a school teacher at Geradgi primary school.
