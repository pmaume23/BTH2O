About BTH2O

BTH2O is a student club founded in Spring 2019 and is an expansion upon Trinity student, Ace McAlister's ‘20 independent water purification and volunteer service project in Ethiopia. The organization raises awareness both on and off campus through a variety of outreach efforts including partnering with other student organizations on campus. Ultimately, our fundraising efforts will result in the implementation of permanent clean water solution to villages in Ethiopia.
